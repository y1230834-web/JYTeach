# 阅读理解 · 押题

> 共 10 篇短文、50 题（选择 + 判断）。先读短文，再做其后的选择题/判断题；判断题对应原文，注意绝对词与否定词。

## Passage 1：Shaking Hands Manners（短文）

> A handshake is not only a way of greeting. It can also show your personality. To make a good first impression, you should know the right shaking hands manners. Stand up and maintain eye contact while shaking hands. If you are seated when someone comes for a handshake, stand up and shake his or her hand. Keep a proper distance. A handshake should be brief and firm. Let go of each other’s hand after 2-3 seconds. Do not press too hard.

## Passage 1 · 选择题

131. What is the best title for the passage?（**A**）
   A. Shaking Hands Manners
   B. How to Write Emails
   C. A Business Plan
   - 解析：全文围绕握手礼仪。

132. A handshake can show _______.（**A**）
   A. your personality
   B. your address
   C. your password
   - 解析：原文说 it can also show your personality。

133. If you are seated when someone comes for a handshake, you should _______.（**B**）
   A. keep seated
   B. stand up
   C. leave the room
   - 解析：原文明确要求 stand up。

134. A proper handshake usually lasts _______.（**A**）
   A. 2-3 seconds
   B. 5 minutes
   C. one hour
   - 解析：原文 after 2-3 seconds。

135. A good handshake should be _______.（**A**）
   A. brief and firm
   B. long and weak
   C. hard and painful
   - 解析：brief and firm 是核心表达。

## Passage 2：Being Punctual（短文）

> Being on time is very important for personal success. To be punctual means to do what you should do on time. Keep your watch exact, or set it two minutes ahead. If you have difficulty getting up, put your alarm clock somewhere you cannot reach from your bed. Try sleeping earlier to allow yourself at least seven hours’ sleep. If you have to be at work at 8:00, tell yourself to arrive at 7:45.

   - 解析：原文 two minutes ahead。

   C. cannot see at all

139. You should sleep earlier to allow yourself _______ sleep.（**A**）

   B. no more than 7 hours’
   C. only 3 hours’

   A. 8:30
   B. 8:00
   C. 7:45

## Passage 4：Effective Meetings（短文）

> Meetings can be weekly, monthly or annually. Some people think meetings are boring and a waste of time. To run an effective meeting, assign roles such as greeter, timekeeper and note taker. Take charge from the start and follow the agenda. Ask participants questions so they can speak their minds. At the end, make sure everyone knows what to do, and send out meeting minutes within a few days.

   - 解析：原文列举 greeter, timekeeper, note taker。

150. Meeting minutes should be sent out _______.（**A**）

## Passage 5：A Gym（短文）

> The gym is “New You”. It is near Oxford Circus. There is a swimming pool and there are many classes. There is a yoga class on Tuesday morning and aerobics classes every evening. There is also a Tai Chi class on Wednesday and Saturday afternoon. There are many running machines and weight machines, but there are not any bicycles. The best time to come is in the evening because there are many trainers to help you. You can pay by cash, credit card or cheque.

## Passage 5 · 选择题

151. The gym is near _______.（**A**）
   A. Oxford Circus
   B. New York University
   C. a post office only
   - 解析：原文 It is near Oxford Circus。

152. A yoga class is on _______.（**A**）
   A. Tuesday morning
   B. Sunday night
   C. Friday evening
   - 解析：原文 Tuesday morning。

## Passage 5 · 判断题

   - 解析：原文说 there are not any bicycles。
154. The best time to come is in the evening.（**√**）
   - 解析：原文直接说明 evening 最好。
155. You can only pay by cash.（**×**）
   - 解析：only 是陷阱，原文有 cash, credit card or cheque。

## Passage 10：Work Plans（短文）

> A work plan is important for a team. It can tell each member what is being done and what should be done next. A good plan includes specific goals, a timeline and each person’s duty. Once you have set a specific timeline, the next step is to take daily action and do your own part. If unexpected events stop you from finishing your tasks, you should change your plan.
