# 交际用语 · 押题（单选题）

> 共 40 题。背常见场景答语，优先选礼貌、自然、符合上下文的回答。点选作答后可看答案与解析。

## 交际用语单选题

1. — Hello, I’m David Chen. Nice to meet you.  — （**C**）
   A. Are you?
   B. Very nice.
   C. Nice to meet you too.
   - 解析：见面介绍的固定回应是 “Nice to meet you too.”。

2. — Sally, （**B**） Mary Brown.  — Nice to meet you. I’m Sally Johnson, the Sales Manager.
   A. what a pleasure
   B. this is our new secretary
   C. thanks a lot
   - 解析：介绍第三人给对方时用 “this is...”。

3. — It’s rather cold in here. Do you mind if I close the window?  — （**B**）
   A. Yes, please.
   B. No, go ahead.
   C. Sure, please.
   - 解析：Do you mind...? 问是否介意；不介意对方关窗，应答 No, go ahead。

4. — Hello, this is Yang Lin speaking.  — （**A**）
   A. This is Carol here.
   B. I am Carol.
   C. Do you know who I am?
   - 解析：电话里自报身份常用 This is... speaking/here，不用 I am...。

5. — （**A**）  — Please call me Mary. That’s my first name.
   A. How shall I address you?
   B. How are you?
   C. Are you Mary?
   - 解析：回答是“请叫我 Mary”，问题应是“我该怎么称呼你”。

6. — Excuse me, where is the post office?  — Sorry, I’m new here.  — （**A**）
   A. Thank you all the same.
   B. What a pity.
   C. Bad luck.
   - 解析：别人帮不上忙，也应礼貌感谢。

7. — Wow, this place is amazing.  — （**B**）
   A. It belongs to my grandparents.
   B. Thank you.
   C. I don’t think so.
   - 解析：对方夸赞场所，通常礼貌回答 Thank you。

8. — May I use your bike for a moment?  — （**C**）
   A. It doesn’t matter.
   B. I have no idea.
   C. By all means.
   - 解析：By all means 表示“当然可以”。

9. — Hey, Tom. You look so pale. What’s wrong?  — （**B**）
   A. It’s none of your business.
   B. I didn’t sleep well last night.
   C. It doesn’t matter.
   - 解析：问身体状况，应说明原因；A 不礼貌，C 不回答问题。

10. — （**B**）  — My mother is retired. My father is a manager.
   A. What are your parents doing right now?
   B. What do your parents do?
   C. Do your parents still work?
   - 解析：回答父母职业，应问 What do your parents do?

11. — Hi, I’m Melinda Smith. Nice to meet you all here.  — （**C**）
   A. Certainly.
   B. Many thanks.
   C. Nice to meet you, too.
   - 解析：Nice to meet you 的标准回应。

12. — Hi, Melinda. Can I make a brief introduction of the office work to you?  — （**B**）
   A. Thanks for calling.
   B. Thank you! It’s very kind of you.
   C. Not too bad.
   - 解析：别人主动介绍工作，应表示感谢。

13. — May I see your tickets, please?  — （**A**）
   A. Sure, here they are.
   B. No, you can’t.
   C. No, they are mine.
   - 解析：检票场景应出示票。

14. — Why don’t you come and join us for a dance?  — （**A**）
   A. I’d love to, but I’m busy now.
   B. I should work.
   C. You choose.
   - 解析：拒绝邀请要礼貌，I’d love to, but... 最自然。

15. — Thank you for inviting me.  — （**B**）
   A. I really had a happy time.
   B. Thank you for coming.
   C. It doesn’t matter.
   - 解析：主人回应客人感谢邀请，应说 Thank you for coming。

16. — Would you like to go to the concert with us this evening?  — （**B**）
   A. No, I already have plans.
   B. I’d love to, but I’m busy tonight.
   C. No, I really don’t like being with you.
   - 解析：礼貌拒绝邀请用 “I’d love to, but...”。

17. — Anything else I can do for you?  — （**A**）
   A. That’s all. Thank you.
   B. You are wrong.
   C. Do it again.
   - 解析：服务场景下表示“不需要别的了”。

18. — Hello. Could I speak to Mike?  — （**A**） I’ll get him to phone.
   A. Hold the line, please.
   B. Don’t speak.
   C. I don’t know you.
   - 解析：电话转接常用 Hold the line, please。

19. — What time does the office open?  — （**A**）
   A. I think it opens at 8.
   B. It opens on Monday.
   C. What do your parents do?
   - 解析：问具体时间，回答 at 8。

20. — Will you show me how to use this software?  — （**A**）
   A. Sure. You’ll master it in no time.
   B. You are welcome.
   C. Sorry, I’m too late for it.
   - 解析：请求演示软件，肯定回答并鼓励对方。

21. — Hello, I’d like to speak to Phil.  — He’s out to lunch now. （**C**）
   A. Sorry, you can’t.
   B. I don’t know.
   C. Would you mind calling back later?
   - 解析：对方不在，礼貌请稍后再打。

22. — Could you please give me a hand?  — （**A**）
   A. No problem.
   B. I don’t like hands.
   C. You are not right.
   - 解析：give me a hand = 帮我一下，No problem 合适。

23. — I have no idea how to use my OA account.  — （**A**）
   A. First, you have to log on.
   B. That is your fault.
   C. It is very expensive.
   - 解析：对方不会使用 OA，应给步骤指导。

24. — How long will the journey take?  — （**A**）
   A. It takes about 3 hours.
   B. It leaves at 8.
   C. It is very long ago.
   - 解析：How long 问持续时间。

25. — Are you ready, folks?  — （**A**）
   A. Yes, I’m ready. I’ll have the vegetable salad.
   B. No, there is no folk.
   C. I don’t like people.
   - 解析：餐厅点餐语境，ready 表示准备好点餐。

26. — How was your trip?  — （**A**）
   A. It was fine, thank you.
   B. It is a car.
   C. I am a trip.
   - 解析：接待访客时询问旅途，回答旅途感受。

27. — Welcome to our company.  — （**A**）
   A. Thank you.
   B. No company.
   C. You are welcome to me.
   - 解析：对欢迎语表达感谢。

28. — Could you tell me how to get to the gym?  — （**A**）
   A. Go straight and turn left.
   B. I am going to gym.
   C. The gym is a person.
   - 解析：问路要给路线。

29. — Can I take a message?  — （**A**）
   A. Yes, please. Tell him to call me back.
   B. No, take a table.
   C. I can’t message.
   - 解析：电话留言场景。

30. — Sorry for being late.  — （**A**）
   A. Never mind.
   B. You are never.
   C. It is a late.
   - 解析：对道歉的礼貌回应。

31. — I’m afraid I can’t finish it today.  — （**A**）
   A. That’s all right.
   B. You can’t afraid.
   C. I am finish.
   - 解析：对无法完成任务的委婉回应。

32. — Do you have any suggestions?  — （**A**）
   A. How about putting a notice on the bulletin board?
   B. I am suggestion.
   C. No, I have a board.
   - 解析：提建议可用 How about doing...。

33. — Can you explain the arrangements in detail?  — （**A**）
   A. Sure, let me explain them one by one.
   B. I don’t detail.
   C. Detail is not arrange.
   - 解析：in detail 表示详细地。

34. — Shall we meet at 9 tomorrow morning?  — （**A**）
   A. That works for me.
   B. It is nine yesterday.
   C. Meet is a noun.
   - 解析：约时间，That works for me 表示可以。

35. — I’d like to book a table for ten.  — （**A**）
   A. Under what name?
   B. Ten is not a table.
   C. You cannot eat a table.
   - 解析：订餐常问以什么名字预订。

36. — I made a mistake in the report.  — （**A**）
   A. It doesn’t matter. Please correct it.
   B. Mistakes are impossible.
   C. You are a report.
   - 解析：工作场景中安慰并提出补救。

37. — Would you mind helping me with the files?  — （**A**）
   A. Of course not.
   B. Yes, I help.
   C. Mind is important.
   - 解析：Would you mind...? 表示请求；Of course not = 不介意，愿意帮。

38. — Thank you for your help.  — （**A**）
   A. You’re welcome.
   B. Thank yourself.
   C. I don’t help.
   - 解析：感谢的固定回应。

39. — I hope more people in our community know this activity.  — （**A**）
   A. You can publish the information on our community website.
   B. People are activity.
   C. Community is not online.
   - 解析：回应发布活动信息的问题。

40. — How shall we publish the notice?  — （**A**）
   A. We can put it online and on each bulletin board.
   B. We shall notice publish.
   C. The notice is impossible.
   - 解析：Unit 8 信息发布场景，website 和 bulletin board 都是重点。
