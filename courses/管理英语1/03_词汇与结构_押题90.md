# 词汇与结构 · 押题（单选题）

> 共 90 题，含高频词组、时态、介词、冠词、定语从句、固定搭配。点选作答后可看答案与解析。

## 词汇与结构单选题

41. I’d like to （**C**） to you our new secretary, Melinda Smith.
   A. meet
   B. welcome
   C. introduce
   - 解析：introduce sb. to sb. = 把某人介绍给某人。

42. People here usually （**A**） each other by their first names.
   A. call
   B. work
   C. hear
   - 解析：call sb. by first name = 直呼某人的名。

43. If you have any questions, we’ll all be （**C**） to help you.
   A. readiness
   B. already
   C. ready
   - 解析：be ready to do = 乐意/准备好做某事。

44. I like the working atmosphere here. It’s very （**C**）.
   A. funny
   B. boring
   C. friendly
   - 解析：working atmosphere 可用 friendly 表示友好氛围。

45. He published the journal （**C**） from 1991 to 2015.
   A. actually
   B. relatively
   C. annually
   - 解析：annually = 每年一次。

46. The work was done （**A**） her instructions.
   A. according to
   B. according with
   C. accordance with
   - 解析：固定搭配 according to。

47. （**A**） wine do you need for the party?
   A. How much
   B. How little
   C. How many
   - 解析：wine 不可数，用 How much。

48. He can （**B**） good English.
   A. talk
   B. speak
   C. say
   - 解析：说某种语言用 speak。

49. He （**B**） lunch in the canteen right now.
   A. has
   B. is having
   C. have
   - 解析：right now 提示现在进行时。

50. Copying files under Linux is similar （**C**） copying files under DOS.
   A. in
   B. with
   C. to
   - 解析：be similar to = 与……相似。

51. Does David （**A**）?
   A. like flying
   B. likes flying
   C. like to flying
   - 解析：助动词 does 后动词用原形；like doing 正确。

52. Mary has been a secretary in this company （**C**） she graduated from Beijing University.
   A. before
   B. when
   C. since
   - 解析：现在完成时常与 since + 过去时间点连用。

53. I’m looking forward to （**C**） together with you.
   A. worked
   B. work
   C. working
   - 解析：look forward to 中 to 是介词，后接动名词。

54. What is your job? I’m （**B**） accountant.
   A. a
   B. an
   C. the
   - 解析：accountant 以元音音素开头，用 an。

55. Keeping eye contact makes the other person （**B**） welcome and comfortable.
   A. to feel
   B. feel
   C. feeling
   - 解析：make sb. do sth. 使某人做某事。

56. Nowadays office work can be done by hand （**C**） very fast speed.
   A. in
   B. on
   C. at
   - 解析：at speed 是固定搭配。

57. Don’t be afraid （**A**） ask a lot of questions.
   A. to
   B. of
   C. on
   - 解析：be afraid to do = 不敢做。

58. How do I （**A**） the gym?
   A. get to
   B. arrive
   C. go
   - 解析：get to + 地点 = 到达某地。

59. We have to （**B**） at the hotel before 6 pm.
   A. check off
   B. check in
   C. check on
   - 解析：入住酒店用 check in。

60. I will give you （**C**） to finish it.
   A. two weeks time
   B. two week’s time
   C. two weeks’ time
   - 解析：两周的时间用复数所有格 two weeks’ time。

61. He has learned English and German. Now he is going to learn （**A**） language.
   A. another
   B. other
   C. the other
   - 解析：another 表示三者或以上中的另一个。

62. Have you （**A**） to say to me?
   A. anything else
   B. else anything
   C. something else
   - 解析：疑问句常用 anything else，else 放在不定代词后。

63. The bus came after they （**A**） for about half an hour.
   A. had been waiting
   B. was waiting
   C. wait
   - 解析：过去某时之前一直等待，用过去完成进行时。

64. I’m a deputy manager. I （**A**） an IT company.
   A. work for
   B. work about
   C. work
   - 解析：work for + 公司 = 在某公司工作。

65. The next train （**B**） the station at 11 o’clock.
   A. has left
   B. leaves
   C. left
   - 解析：列车时刻表常用一般现在时表将来。

66. Smile a lot and be （**C**） friendly as possible.
   A. very
   B. so
   C. as
   - 解析：as...as possible = 尽可能……。

67. A well （**A**） job description can answer many questions.
   A. written
   B. write
   C. wrote
   - 解析：well-written = 写得好的，复合形容词。

68. It is important to find a （**B**） between work life and home life.
   A. way
   B. balance
   C. blank
   - 解析：balance between A and B = A 与 B 的平衡。

69. The teacher showed the students （**C**） the laboratory.
   A. on
   B. at
   C. around
   - 解析：show sb. around = 带某人参观。

70. Please hand your homework （**A**） time.
   A. in
   B. on
   C. by
   - 解析：hand in = 提交；in time = 及时。此题空在 hand 后，选 in。

71. Within the team, a work plan can tell each member what is being （**C**）.
   A. do
   B. did
   C. done
   - 解析：被动语态 be being done。

72. This would prevent companies （**A**） creating new jobs.
   A. from
   B. for
   C. to
   - 解析：prevent sb. from doing。

73. On our （**B**）, we were told that our rooms had not been reserved.
   A. arrive
   B. arrival
   C. arriving
   - 解析：on arrival = 到达时。

74. Please pay （**A**） to what was discussed yesterday.
   A. attention
   B. attend
   C. attitude
   - 解析：pay attention to = 注意。

75. To （**B**） the ice, after introduction, you can ask about their journey.
   A. attack
   B. break
   C. make
   - 解析：break the ice = 打破僵局。

76. I heard Lily （**A**） the song once.
   A. sing
   B. sang
   C. sings
   - 解析：hear sb. do sth. 听到某人做过某事。

77. Are you still looking （**A**） a job?
   A. for
   B. up
   C. after
   - 解析：look for = 寻找。

78. This book gives some useful （**C**） on how to save money.
   A. taps
   B. tapes
   C. tips
   - 解析：tips = 建议，小窍门。

79. Everyone is very （**A**） to this boy.
   A. friendly
   B. fast
   C. fine
   - 解析：be friendly to sb. = 对某人友好。

80. （**C**） eye contact is very important in western culture.
   A. Keep
   B. Kept
   C. Keeping
   - 解析：动名词短语作主语。

81. After you are （**B**） with all these jobs, we will discuss it.
   A. friendly
   B. familiar
   C. fine
   - 解析：be familiar with = 熟悉。

82. Can you show me how to apply （**B**） this job?
   A. to
   B. for
   C. with
   - 解析：apply for a job = 申请工作。

83. She doesn’t have enough funds to （**A**） her design.
   A. carry out
   B. end up
   C. make sure
   - 解析：carry out = 执行，实施。

84. Do you want to fly first class or （**B**） class?
   A. economic
   B. economy
   C. economical
   - 解析：economy class = 经济舱。

85. （**A**） means to do what you should do on time.
   A. Being punctual
   B. To be late
   C. Being careless
   - 解析：punctual = 守时的。

86. I’d like to book a table for ten _______ 7 p.m. _______ December 24th.（**C**）
   A. in; at
   B. in; in
   C. at; on
   - 解析：具体时刻用 at，具体日期用 on。

87. Can you explain your arrangements （**B**） detail?
   A. of
   B. in
   C. on
   - 解析：in detail = 详细地。

88. It may be helpful to set your watch just two minutes （**A**）.
   A. ahead
   B. before
   C. forward
   - 解析：two minutes ahead = 提前两分钟。

89. Jack （**B**） to see you. He’ll be here soon.
   A. comes
   B. is coming
   C. came
   - 解析：is coming 表示即将到来。

90. Everyone in these schools （**A**） English well.
   A. speaks
   B. talks
   C. says
   - 解析：everyone 作主语谓语用三单；说语言用 speak。

91. A secretary must （**A**） a big pile of files and correspondence.
   A. deal with
   B. agree with
   C. get along with
   - 解析：deal with = 处理。

92. Please see the suggestions below to （**C**） that your files are not lost.
   A. be sure
   B. make out
   C. make sure
   - 解析：make sure = 确保。

93. _______ you deal with the data files, _______ familiar you get with them.（**A**）
   A. The more; the more
   B. If; more
   C. Because; more
   - 解析：the more..., the more... = 越……越……。

94. You can pick a specific time and place and ask them （**C**） they want to go.
   A. that
   B. where
   C. if
   - 解析：ask sb. if... = 询问某人是否……。

95. Charles regretted （**B**） the TV set last year.
   A. to buy
   B. buying
   C. of buying
   - 解析：regret doing = 后悔做过某事。

96. The manager will not （**C**） us to use his car.
   A. let
   B. make
   C. allow
   - 解析：allow sb. to do；let 后不接 to。

97. Neither John （**A**） his father was able to wake up early.
   A. nor
   B. or
   C. and
   - 解析：neither...nor... = 既不……也不……。

98. （**A**） these honors, he received a sum of money.
   A. Besides
   B. Beside
   C. Except
   - 解析：besides = 除……之外还；beside = 在旁边。

99. A good tour guide tells visitors （**B**）.
   A. what during the trip they couldn’t miss
   B. what they couldn’t miss during the trip
   C. what couldn’t miss they during the trip
   - 解析：宾语从句用陈述语序。

100. It was on the beach （**A**） Miss White found the kid.
   A. that
   B. it
   C. which
   - 解析：强调句型 It was...that...。

101. The man （**A**） is speaking at the meeting is a worker.
   A. who
   B. whom
   C. where
   - 解析：先行词是人，关系词在从句中作主语，用 who。

102. The woman （**B**） they wanted to visit is a teacher.
   A. where
   B. whom
   C. why
   - 解析：先行词是人，关系词作宾语，用 whom/who/that。

103. I know the girl （**B**） mother is a teacher.
   A. who
   B. whose
   C. which
   - 解析：表示“她的妈妈”，用 whose。

104. This is a truck （**A**） is made in China.
   A. which
   B. whom
   C. where
   - 解析：先行词是物，关系词作主语，用 which/that。

105. Tom is the cleverest boy （**B**） I have ever known.
   A. which
   B. that
   C. whom
   - 解析：先行词被最高级修饰，通常用 that。

106. This is the first play （**A**） I have seen here.
   A. that
   B. who
   C. where
   - 解析：先行词被序数词 first 修饰，用 that。

107. Everything （**B**） we saw in this film was true.
   A. which
   B. that
   C. who
   - 解析：先行词是不定代词 everything，用 that。

108. This is the very book （**A**） belongs to him.
   A. that
   B. which
   C. whom
   - 解析：先行词被 the very 修饰，常用 that。

109. Who is the girl （**B**） is standing under the tree?
   A. which
   B. that
   C. whom
   - 解析：主句已有 who，定语从句关系代词常用 that。

110. I have never heard of the people and things （**C**） you talked about.
   A. which
   B. who
   C. that
   - 解析：先行词既有人又有物，用 that。

111. This is the boy with （**B**） he talked.
   A. who
   B. whom
   C. that
   - 解析：介词 with 提前，指人用 whom。

112. This is the ring on （**A**） she spent one thousand dollars.
   A. which
   B. that
   C. who
   - 解析：介词 on 提前，指物用 which。

113. I still remember the day （**A**） I first met him.
   A. when
   B. where
   C. which
   - 解析：先行词是时间，且从句缺时间状语，用 when。

114. This is the place （**B**） I was born.
   A. when
   B. where
   C. why
   - 解析：先行词是地点，且从句缺地点状语，用 where。

115. This is the reason （**A**） I was late.
   A. why
   B. where
   C. when
   - 解析：先行词 reason，表示原因，用 why。

116. The dictionary is the only place （**A**） success comes before work.
   A. where
   B. which
   C. that
   - 解析：从句主干完整，缺地点状语，用 where。

117. My brother, （**B**） lives in New York, is a doctor.
   A. that
   B. who
   C. which
   - 解析：非限制性定语从句不用 that，指人用 who。

118. I don’t like the way （**A**） he looks at me.
   A. that
   B. whom
   C. whose
   - 解析：the way 后关系词作方式状语时可用 that/in which/省略。

119. The old man has two sons, both of （**B**） are lawyers.
   A. who
   B. whom
   C. that
   - 解析：介词 of 后指人用 whom。

120. I’d like a room （**A**） window looks out over the sea.
   A. whose
   B. which
   C. where
   - 解析：表示房间的窗户，用 whose window。

121. The newly built cafe, the walls of （**A**） are painted light green, is peaceful.
   A. which
   B. that
   C. whom
   - 解析：the walls of which = whose walls，指物用 which。

122. Those （**A**） want to visit the exhibition sign your name here.
   A. who
   B. which
   C. where
   - 解析：those who... 是固定结构，表示“那些……的人”。

123. The book （**B**） I bought yesterday is interesting.
   A. what
   B. that
   C. where
   - 解析：先行词 book，关系词作宾语，可用 that/which。

124. The doctor （**A**） you are looking for is in the room.
   A. whom
   B. where
   C. why
   - 解析：look for 的宾语是人，用 whom/who/that。

125. The teacher for （**B**） you are waiting has come.
   A. who
   B. whom
   C. that
   - 解析：介词 for 提前，指人只能用 whom。

126. The room in （**A**） there are many books is mine.
   A. which
   B. that
   C. who
   - 解析：介词 in 提前，指物用 which。

127. The boy （**A**） composition won the first prize is the youngest.
   A. whose
   B. who
   C. whom
   - 解析：composition 属于 boy，用 whose。

128. He prefers to eat the rice （**A**） is grown in the south.
   A. which
   B. who
   C. where
   - 解析：先行词 rice 是物，关系词作主语，用 which/that。

129. This book is for students （**A**） native language is not English.
   A. whose
   B. whom
   C. which
   - 解析：native language 属于 students，用 whose。

130. The less clear you are about what you want to do, （**A**） effective your plan will be.
   A. the less
   B. less
   C. the least
   - 解析：the less..., the less... 结构。
